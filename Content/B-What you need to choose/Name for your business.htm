Name for your business

 

The name of your business must include your first name and your family name.

When you register your business with CEIDG, you will need to select your trade name. The name must include at least your first name and last name. You need to observe the following rules.

  • The required order is: first name, last name. You can add your middle name, if you want.

Possible options are:

  • John Doe
  • John J. Doe
  • John James Doe
  • The spelling must be the same as in your ID document, except that you do not need to use capital letters only. Remember that capitalization should be correct.

 

Possible options are:

  • John Doe
  • JOHN DOE

But not: * jOHN DoE etc.

  • If used within a phrase in Polish, it should be in a nominative case

 

 

John Doe Nauka angielskiego,

But not *Angielski z Johnem Doe

  • There should be a single space before the first name and the last name.
  • You can add other elements to your first and last name to describe your business

 

It could be a short description of your services – John Doe Nauka angielskiego.

It could be the place where you run your business – John Doe Warszawa Mokotów.

It can be your alias, your slogan, or any element that can highlight your business objective – John Doe - English for you.

 

You can add elements of your name in English (or any other language), but it is recommended to keep them short and simple. You will need to dictate the name of your business to your partners (for example, when you are asking for an invoice), so long and difficult names can cause problems.

John Doe Nauka angielskiego is better

than John Doe Professional English Instruction

 

 

You can have only one entry in CEIDG for your first name and last name, but you can run various types of business under this single entry.

 

 

  1. Choose a name for your business meeting the above criteria.
  2. Enter it in field of your registration application.
