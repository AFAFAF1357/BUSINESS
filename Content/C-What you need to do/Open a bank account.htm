Is a bank account required ?

When running your business, you can use either a business bank account, or your private bank account. If you choose the second option, though, you must be the only holder of your private account.

There are some transactions that must not be settled in cash. In such cases a bank account is required to effect payment.

A businessperson must not pay in cash, when:

  • the transaction is with another business
  • a one-off value of the transaction exceeds PLN 15,000, regardless of the number of payments made.

You will need a bank account to pay your taxes and social security contributions.

 

If in at least one year in the last two years you had employed fewer than 10 persons and your annual net revenue has not exceeded EUR 2 million, you do not need a bank account - you can pay your taxes and social security contributions at the post office.

You will need a business bank account (not your private one), if:

  • you are a VAT payer – only business bank account numbers are pubished in the so-called white list of VAT payers
  • use the split payment mechanism

In short, it may be convenient to have a business bank account, even when it is not mandatory.
